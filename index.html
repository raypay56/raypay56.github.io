Benefits

  • 79% of employees cited a competitive benefits plan as an influencer, and 77% cited cost
  • 72% of workers are satisfied, but 60% are still looking around for a new job with higher wages
  • 50% of employees said they have left a position to escape a bad manager
  • 25% of employees would require at least a 20% increase in salary to justify making a move
  • More than 60% of employees would take a job with a lower salary for better benefits
  • 42% of businesses report that pension provision has a positive impact on employee retention
  • 15% of employee candidates don’t ask about benefits at all during the interview process
  • 51% of employees would quit their job if training was not offered

Retirement Benefits

  • 69% of small business owners don’t feel fully prepared for retirement
  • 59% of small business owners don’t think they can afford a retirement plan
  • 44% of small business owners don’t see the need or benefit to offering a retirement plan
  • 69% say a firm’s pension scheme is an important factor when looking for a new job
  • 86% of people want to retire at or before 65, but only 37% believe this to be an option
  • 20% of people believe they will never be able to retire due to poor financial planning
  • 40% of workers don’t think they’ll be able to retire until 70 or older
  • Two-thirds of millennials had not saved a single dollar for retirement savings
